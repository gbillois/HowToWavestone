How scoring works

Each control is rated Not started (0%), Planned (25%), In progress (50%), or Implemented (100%); mark items that genuinely don’t apply as N/A to exclude them. The overall score is the mean of all in-scope controls. Time-horizon bars roll up only the controls tagged to each horizon, so you can see whether your immediate This-week actions are truly done even while longer-term work is in flight. A program is considered Mythos-Ready at 90%+ — the CSA frames this as minimum viable resilience, not perfection. Answers are saved in your browser (localStorage) only; nothing is sent anywhere.
